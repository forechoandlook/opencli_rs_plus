工具列表:
- **ask**: Send a prompt to Gemini and get response
- **canvas**: Use Gemini Canvas for code execution and text editing
- **deep-research**: Run deep research on a topic with Gemini
- **for-you**: Get personalized For You recommendations from Gemini
- **gems**: List and manage Gemini Gems (custom AI agents)
- **history**: List Gemini conversation history
- **image**: Generate an image with Gemini and save to ~/Downloads
- **mystuff**: List saved content from Gemini My stuff
- **music**: Generate music with Gemini
- **status**: Check Gemini login and account status
- **video**: Generate video with Gemini
- **write**: Use Gemini's Write anything tool
