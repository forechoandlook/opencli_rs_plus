工具列表:
- **background**: Generate AI background for a product/object image (5 credits)
- **edit**: Yollomi AI edit
- **face-swap**: Yollomi AI face-swap
- **generate**: Generate images with AI (text-to-image or image-to-image)
- **models**: List available Yollomi AI models (image, video, tools)
- **object-remover**: Yollomi AI object-remover
- **remove-bg**: Yollomi AI remove-bg
- **restore**: Yollomi AI restore
- **try-on**: Yollomi AI try-on
- **upscale**: Yollomi AI upscale
- **upload**: Upload an image or video to Yollomi (returns URL for other commands)
- **video**: Generate videos with AI (text-to-video or image-to-video)
