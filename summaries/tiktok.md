工具列表:
- comment: Comment on a TikTok video

- follow: Follow a TikTok user
- friends: Get TikTok friend suggestions
- live: Browse live streams on TikTok
- like: Like a TikTok video
- notifications: Get TikTok notifications (likes, comments, mentions, followers)
- profile: Get TikTok user profile info
- save: Add a TikTok video to Favorites
- search: Search TikTok videos
- unfollow: Unfollow a TikTok user
- unlike: Unlike a TikTok video
- unsave: Remove a TikTok video from Favorites
- user: Get recent videos from a TikTok user
- following: List accounts you follow on TikTok
