工具列表:
- **book**: View book details on WeRead
- **highlights**: List your highlights (underlines) in a book
- **notebooks**: List books that have highlights or notes
- **notes**: List your notes (thoughts) on a book
- **ranking**: WeRead book rankings by category
- **search**: Search books on WeRead
- **shelf**: List books on your WeRead bookshelf
